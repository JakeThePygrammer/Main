Skopje

Skopje - Skopje is one of my favorite cities. I adore its interesting blend of traditional and modern architecture, from the old bazaar
from ottoman times, to the modern buildings like the ones in Novo Lisiche or the ones built by Kenzo Tange.

Skopje is also the place where I have lived most of my life. It's sad seeing my home being ruined by corrupt politicians and people ruining the city.
I think that Skopje is due for a change.
