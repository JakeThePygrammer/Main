Recent news :

Modernization of educational institutions

The MSG government is actively working on constructing, renovating and improving on the educational standard in MSG. By 2029, we plan for all schools
to only have one shift, to not disrupt the sleep of students. The addition of classes for modern technologies and advanced machine learning
are preparing the next generation for progress. The MSG university is under construction, with an expected construction date of 2030.

Train delays due to construction on MSG - MSG industry rail lines

Due to heavy construction works on the MSG - MSG industry line, all trains will be running on reduced speeds. We thank you for understanding.

Construction works on MSG - MSG industry highway

The construction works on the MSG - MSG industry highway are going to plan. The expected completion date is currently set at Summer 2027.

Construction works on new residential neighborhoods and how to apply for water.

Due to heavy construction works on new residential neighborhoods, water services will be suspended from the Genex neighborhood from 22.02.2026 at 00:00
to 23.02.2026 at 12:30. You may go to any Tinon/Tion store and provide your residence card to get your water rations. We thank you for understanding.

Sheltering drill 01.02.2026.

The city-wide sheltering drill will commence on the first of February 2026. This drill encompasses all residents. It will last 2 hours. Event organizers
have been alerted. If you have not been alerted and prepared, contact us.

Opening preparations of the new hotel and city beach

The city hotel and the city beach are in the final phases before opening! The finishing touches are being added as our teams work day and night to get it all ready.
