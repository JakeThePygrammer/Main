About our city

Our city

MSG, the capital of the country of MSG, is located in the Osoj river basin, with a temperate climate, with
long summers and short winters. The mountains around MSG are ideal for skiing. The first settlements in MSG
were over 3000 years ago, making it an archaeological hotspot. MSG has gone through a complex history, with many
historical events.

Most of MSG was constructed after WW2, after nazi occupation ended. MSG was successfully freed by general Jakov on 29th of November 1945.
MSG then switched to a socialist way of government, with rapid construction and modernization of the city. Now, MSG is a large industrial and
commercial hub, with rapid growth.

The flag of MSG was designed during WW2, as a banner for the country.


Our main external consulate is located in Belgrade.
